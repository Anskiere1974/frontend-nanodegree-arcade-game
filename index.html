How to play!

Our hero starts in the grass on the bottom. He has to cross the streets - avoiding all those nasty bugs - to reach the lovely princess.

You can move by using the up, down, left and right arrow buttons.

If you collide with a bug, you will lose a life. If you have zero lives you will lose the game.

Everytime you reach the princess you score one point. If you get five points, you win and the lovely princess will be your prom date.

By the way: The font - Press Start 2P - used with this project is a pixel based font based on the font design from 1980s Namco arcade games.
